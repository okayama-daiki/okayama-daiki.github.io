## Who am I?

I am a programmer in Japan and occasionally take on software development contracts from [Dakken LLC.](https://dakken.co.jp), a student-run company focusing on data analysis and software development. I received my Bachelor's degree in Social Information Science from [University of Hyogo](https://www.u-hyogo.ac.jp/english/). I am currently a Master's student at [Graduate School of Information Science, University of Hyogo](https://u-hyogo-gsis.org/en/), majoring in _online exploration_.
